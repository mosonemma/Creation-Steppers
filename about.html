Who We Are

Empowering youth to transform communities through innovation, leadership, and advocacy.

Creation Steppers Youth Development Initiative (CSYDI) is a community-based organization dedicated to building skills, creativity, and empowerment among young people in Uganda. Through art, education, and social action, we inspire transformation and sustainable impact.

Our Objectives

Empower Youth

Equip young people with entrepreneurial, leadership, and life skills to create sustainable livelihoods.

Promote Innovation

Encourage creativity and innovation through projects, technology, and arts programs.

Community Transformation

Engage communities in health awareness, education campaigns, and advocacy for positive change.

Environmental Sustainability

Promote eco-friendly practices among the youth and local communities.

Our Journey

2011

Formation

Started as Creation Steppers Music Empire to use art for community transformation.

2015

Expansion

Introduced drama and dance projects addressing social issues like FGM and early marriages.

2018

Official Registration

Became Creation Steppers Youth Development Initiative (CSYDI) — expanding to skills training and advocacy.

2023

Community Impact

Launched new projects focusing on innovation, digital skills, and sustainability across Uganda.

Our Mission

CSYDI exists to efficiently and effectively provide, support, promote and implement integrated community development interventions that enhance the knowledge, skills and rights of the youth to sustainably utilize the available resources, services and technologies to better their socioeconomic status.

Our Vision

An empowered youth for change.
