Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Creation Steppers Youth Development Initiative (CSYDI) — our work, how to get involved, and how to support youth empowerment in Uganda.

CSYDI exists to efficiently and effectively provide, support, promote and implement integrated community development interventions that enhance the knowledge, skills and rights of the youth to sustainably utilize the available resources, services and technologies to better their socioeconomic status.

We run vocational skills training, menstrual health & FGM awareness campaigns, youth leadership workshops, community outreach and livelihood support activities.

Send us a message through the Contacts page with your skills and availability. We welcome in-person and remote volunteers for events, training or admin support.

You can make one-time or recurring donations via the Donate page. We also accept in-kind support for training materials and event refreshments.

Our program activities take place across districts in Uganda. Contact us on the Contacts page for exact locations and upcoming events.
